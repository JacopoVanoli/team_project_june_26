# Forecasting heat-related mortality
## Annual CEPH team project 2026


## Aim: 

Provide distric-level mortality assessments of heat-related mortality (>mmt) using forecast data from 2km MeteoSchweiz forecasts (1-5days). Heat is defined as the risk of mortality in summer (May-Sep) beyond the 95th percentile of the temperature distribution and moderate heat as temperatures below but above the mmt. Temperature crossbasis similar to "The footpring of human-induced..." Vicedo-Cabrera 2022.

## Target audience: 

Simple and understandable, non-scientific audience

## Communication: 

Temperature exposure. Groups of heat-related risks in categories as anomalies.
